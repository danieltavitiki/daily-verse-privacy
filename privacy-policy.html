Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 6, 2025

Simple Summary: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Everything stays on your device. Your privacy is 100% protected.

Introduction

Daily Verse Wallpaper ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Don't Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The App does not:

  • Require account creation or login
  • Collect any personal data
  • Use analytics or tracking
  • Monitor user behavior
  • Access your contacts, location, or other personal information
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS SwiftData. This includes:

  • Bible verses and translations
  • Your customization preferences (background styles)
  • Verse viewing history
  • Notification settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else. When you uninstall the app, all data is automatically deleted from your device.

Permissions

The App requests the following optional permissions:

Photo Library Access

  • Used only when you choose to save a wallpaper to your Photos
  • Only requested when you tap the "Save Wallpaper" button
  • You can use the app without granting this permission
  • We never access your photos - only save new wallpapers you create

Notifications

  • Used only for optional daily verse reminders at 8:00 AM
  • Completely optional - you choose whether to enable it
  • Can be enabled or disabled anytime in the app Settings
  • Local notifications only - no data is sent anywhere

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional "Pro Unlock" in-app purchase to access additional Bible translations. This purchase is processed entirely by Apple through their StoreKit system. Apple's Privacy Policy applies to all payment information. We do not see, store, or have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App uses:

  • Apple StoreKit - For processing in-app purchases (Apple's Privacy Policy applies)

The App does NOT use:

  • Analytics services (no Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media SDKs
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any other third-party tracking or data collection

Children's Privacy

Daily Verse Wallpaper is suitable for all ages, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any information from anyone, we don't collect information from children. Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is completely protected.

Bible Content

The App displays Bible verses from various translations. All Bible content is in the public domain or used under appropriate licensing. No personal information is associated with viewing this content.

Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing to:

  • Access - All your data is already on your device
  • Delete - Simply uninstall the app
  • Export - Your data is stored locally in standard iOS formats
  • Correct - You have full control of your local data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We will notify users of significant changes through the App Store update notes.
